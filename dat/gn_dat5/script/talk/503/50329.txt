

Focus: Focus Skills are related with a Puppet's Focus stat.\b
The higher the Focus Attack (FoAtk) stat, the greater the damage to the enemy.\b
Also, the higher the Focus Defense (FoDef) stat, the lesser the damage to the enemy.\b
They're displayed with RED letters.\b
\cSpread: Spread Skills are related with a Puppet's Spread stat.\b
The higher the Spread Attack (SpAtk) stat, the greater the damage to the enemy.\b
Also, the higher the Spread Defense (SpDef) stats, the lesser the damage to the enemy.\b
They're displayed with BLUE letters.
